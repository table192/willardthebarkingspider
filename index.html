About the Book

Willard the Barking Spider is a charming children’s story about courage, curiosity, and friendship. Willard has lived his whole life in a barn, spinning webs and watching the world from afar. One day, he gathers his courage, climbs to the top of a hill, and discovers a vibrant park full of playful dogs and laughing children.

Determined to make friends, Willard watches a gentle dog named Butch and three children, Logan, Maurizio, and Amy. Hoping to fit in, he finds a baseball cap, a pair of sneakers, and a shiny bottle cap left behind by the kids. Using the bottle cap like a mirror, he practices barking until, to his delight, a real bark comes out.

With newfound confidence, Willard approaches the children and Butch. They smile at his bravery, welcome him into their games, and show readers that being different is something to celebrate. Along with the story, readers can now enjoy extra activity pages, including puzzles and coloring pages that extend the fun beyond the final page.
